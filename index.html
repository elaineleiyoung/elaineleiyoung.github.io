ABOUT ME

I am a freshman student at Boston University pursuing a major in computer science and minor in economics. I'm interested in data science, software development, and AI.

In my free time, I enjoy playing a round of golf or exploring the city. My favorite subjects include math and computer science, but I also enjoy reading and writing.
